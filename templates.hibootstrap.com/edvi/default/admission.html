Admission Rules

At Damotak International School, we have established a clear and comprehensive admission process to ensure that every prospective student is given fair consideration. Our admission rules are as follows:

  1. Application Form: Complete the official application form available on our website or at the school office.
  2. Document Submission: Submit required documents, including previous academic records, birth certificate, and proof of residence.
  3. Interview: Schedule an interview with the admissions committee. This allows us to understand the studentâ€™s interests and aspirations.
  4. Entrance Assessment: Depending on the grade level, students may be required to complete an entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness.
  5. Notification: Parents will receive admission decisions via email within two weeks of the interview and assessment.
  6. Enrollment: Accepted students must complete the enrollment process, including payment of fees and submission of additional documentation.

We encourage families to reach out to our admissions office for any questions or clarifications during the process. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all students.
