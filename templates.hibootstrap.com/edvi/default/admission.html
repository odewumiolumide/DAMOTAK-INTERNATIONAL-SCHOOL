Admission Process

Admission into Damotak International School is carried out strictly at the school premises through a clear and well-structured process.

01

Collection of Admission Form

Parents or guardians are required to visit the school office to obtain and complete the official admission form.

02

Submission of Documents

Completed forms must be submitted at the school office along with the childâ€™s previous school records, birth certificate, and passport photographs.

03

Entrance Assessment

Prospective students will be assessed in subjects suitable for their intended class to determine academic readiness.

04

Admission Approval

Successful candidates will be informed in person or through a phone call after the assessment has been reviewed.

05

Registration & Resumption

Admitted students complete registration by paying the required school fees and are given a resumption date.
